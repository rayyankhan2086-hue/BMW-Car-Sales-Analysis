# BMW-Car-Sales-Analysis

This BMW Car Sales Analysis report provides an overview of total revenue, sales volume, and average price across different models, regions, years, and fuel types. The dashboard shows that revenue is fairly evenly distributed across regions, with Asia and Europe generating slightly higher revenue. Sales volume is highest among popular models like the 3 Series, 5 Series, and X series. The yearly trend indicates steady revenue growth with some fluctuations over time. Fuel type performance is balanced, with Hybrid, Petrol, Electric, and Diesel contributing almost equally. The report also highlights how price impacts sales volume and compares revenue by car color, showing relatively similar performance across colors. Overall, the dashboard helps identify top-performing models, regions, and fuel categories while allowing interactive filtering for deeper analysis.
